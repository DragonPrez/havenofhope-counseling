NOTICE TO CLIENTS

The Texas Behavioral Health Executive Council (BHEC) investigates and prosecutes professional misconduct committed by:

  • Licensed Professional Counselors
  • Marriage and Family Therapists
  • Psychologists and Psychological Associates
  • Social Workers
  • Licensed Specialists in School Psychology

Although not every complaint against or dispute with a licensee involves professional misconduct, the Executive Council will provide information about how to file a complaint.
